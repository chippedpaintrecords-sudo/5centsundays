ABOUT

My name is Ben, and I live in Maine. I started playing guitar in 2003 at the age of 8, and started writing music at 14. Over the years, I've learned many other instruments, and learned to record as well. I've always been a huge fan of 90's post-hardcore/emo, 80's thrash metal, and hardcore punk, so the music I make has elements from all of those genres, but with a grunge/nu-metal twist. Occasionally I make something that's more mellowed out. Everything here is written and produced by me in my living room.
